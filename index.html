Local Issues We Champion

Childcare and Family Support

We recognize the importance of quality childcare and support for families. We advocate for increased access to affordable childcare resources, making our community more family-friendly.

Economic Growth

We believe that a strong local economy is the foundation of a vibrant community. We are committed to turning our area into a hub for businesses, creating job opportunities, and fostering economic prosperity for all.

Housing and Homeownership

Affordable housing is a fundamental right. We work towards policies and initiatives that make homeownership accessible to everyone in our community, helping residents achieve their dream of owning a home.

Safe Communities

Safety is a top priority. We are determined to address issues like anti-social behavior to ensure our streets are safe and well-maintained.
We want children to play without worry, and families to enjoy peace of mind.
