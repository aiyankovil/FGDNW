Meet Our Candidates for the Local Elections

At Fine Gael - Dublin North West, we are proud to present our candidates for the upcoming local elections. Our candidates embody the spirit of commitment, dedication, and passion for serving the community. Let's meet them:

Arnold Guo – Candidate for Ballymun-Finglas Local Electoral Area

Arnold Guo is a name synonymous with dedication and community service in the Ballymun-Finglas area. As a Fine Gael candidate, Arnold brings a unique blend of passion and practical insight to the political arena. His commitment to the residents of this area is rooted in his deep understanding of their needs and aspirations.

A proud resident of Broombridge, Arnold’s connection with the community is personal and profound. His journey in community service is characterized by his relentless pursuit of progress and development for the area. Arnold’s understanding of the local issues comes from his close interactions with the people and his eagerness to bring about positive change.

Arnold envisions a future where the Ballymun-Finglas area thrives as a hub of opportunity, inclusivity, and growth. He is particularly focused on addressing key local issues like housing, community development, and public services. His approach is collaborative, seeking to work alongside residents to build a community that reflects their hopes and needs.

Declan Flanagan – Councillor, Dublin City Council

Declan Flanagan is not just a representative; he is a testament to what persistent and effective leadership can achieve. With over a decade of experience on the Dublin City Council, Declan has proven himself as a Councillor who not only listens but acts.

Declan’s tenure on the Dublin City Council is marked by notable achievements and tireless advocacy for his constituents. His work ethic and commitment to the community have made him a respected and trusted figure in local politics.

Declan’s focus as a Councillor has always been on the betterment of the local community. He is a champion of sustainable urban development, public safety, and enhancing local amenities. His continued goal is to ensure that the voices of his constituents are heard and their needs are met effectively.
